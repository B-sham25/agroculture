Amaranth Seeds

Premium amaranth seeds grow vibrant, nutrient-dense leaves and tender stems packed with iron, vitamins, and mild earthy flavor. These heat-tolerant greens produce abundant foliage perfect for callaloo soup, stir-fries, salads, or as a spinach substitute, thriving in warm, sunny Mauritian gardens with quick regrowth.

Mode of Use

Sow amaranth seeds 0.5–1 cm deep in fertile, well-drained soil or trays, spacing 2–5 cm apart in rows 30–40 cm apart. Keep soil moist and warm (20–35°C) for fast germination in 4–10 days. Thin seedlings to 15–20 cm apart, provide full sun, water consistently, and harvest young leaves/stems starting at 3–5 weeks for continuous cuttings and multiple harvests.

Warning Label

Keep out of reach of children. Store in a cool dry place. Do not ingest.
