Fungisigin 500 SC

Fungisigin 500 SC is a systemic carbamate fungicide/bactericide with thiophanate-methyl 500 g/L. Broad-spectrum control of fungal diseases like powdery mildew, leaf spots, blights, and anthracnose on vegetables, fruits, and ornamentals, offering preventive and curative action for healthier Mauritian crops.

Mode of Use

Dilute per label rates (typically 1–2 ml/L water). Shake bottle well, mix into sprayer tank, and apply as foliar spray at first sign of disease or preventively. Ensure thorough coverage of leaves and stems, avoid application during rain or high heat, and reapply every 7–14 days as needed. Wear full PPE during mixing and spraying.

Warning Label

POISON – WHO Class III. Keep out of reach of children. Toxic if swallowed or inhaled. Wear gloves, mask, goggles, and protective clothing. Do not eat, drink, or smoke while handling. Wash thoroughly after use. In case of exposure, rinse affected area and seek medical advice if symptoms persist.
