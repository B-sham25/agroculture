Cororothrin 2.5 EC Insecticide

Cororothrin 2.5 EC is a powerful pyrethroid insecticide with deltamethrin 25 g/L for fast knockdown of chewing and sucking pests. Effective against aphids, caterpillars, beetles, and flies on vegetables, fruits, sugarcane, and field crops, providing long-lasting protection in Mauritian agriculture.

Mode of Use

Dilute according to crop label rates (usually 1–2 ml/L water). Fill sprayer tank with water first, add measured Cororothrin, agitate well, and apply as a foliar spray during early pest infestation. Use fine mist nozzle for even coverage, avoid spraying during flowering or windy conditions, and reapply every 7–14 days as needed. Wear full PPE and follow all safety precautions.

Warning Label

POISON – WHO Class II. Keep out of reach of children. Toxic if swallowed, inhaled, or absorbed through skin. Wear gloves, mask, goggles, and protective clothing. Do not eat, drink, or smoke while handling. Wash thoroughly after use. In case of poisoning, seek immediate medical help and show this label.
