Kim Choy Seeds

Premium kim choy seeds grow tender, juicy stems and leaves with a mild, sweet flavor. These fast-growing greens produce abundant, nutrient-rich shoots perfect for stir-fries, soups, salads, or steaming, thriving in warm, humid Mauritian conditions with quick harvests.

Mode of Use

Sow kim choy seeds 0.5–1 cm deep in fertile, well-drained soil or trays, spacing 2–3 cm apart in rows 20–30 cm apart. Keep soil moist and warm (20–30°C) for fast germination in 4–7 days. Thin seedlings to 10–15 cm apart, provide full sun to partial shade, and harvest young shoots at 25–40 days for tender, flavorful greens with multiple cuttings possible.

Warning Label

Keep out of reach of children. Store in a cool dry place. Do not ingest.
