Carrot Seeds

High-quality carrot seeds produce crisp, sweet, nutrient-packed roots rich in vitamin A. They thrive in loose, well-drained soil with full sun, developing straight, vibrant orange (or colorful) carrots perfect for fresh eating, cooking, or storage.

Mode of Use

Sow carrot seeds 0.5–1 cm deep in loose, well-drained soil, spacing them 2–3 cm apart in rows 20–30 cm apart. Keep soil consistently moist during germination (7–21 days), thin seedlings to 5–10 cm apart for straight roots, and place in full sun for best growth and color development.

Warning Label

Keep out of reach of children. Store in a cool dry place. Do not ingest.
