White Radish Seeds

Premium white radish seeds grow long, crisp roots with mild, refreshing flavor and high water content. These fast-growing plants produce large, smooth daikon-style radishes perfect for salads, pickles, soups, stir-fries, or grated fresh, thriving in cool to moderate Mauritian conditions with loose soil.

Mode of Use

Sow white radish seeds 1–2 cm deep in loose, well-drained soil, spacing 3–5 cm apart in rows 30–40 cm apart. Keep soil consistently moist and cool (15–25°C) for quick germination in 4–7 days. Thin seedlings to 10–15 cm apart for straight, uniform roots, provide full sun to partial shade, and harvest at 40–60 days when roots reach 20–30 cm long for crisp texture and mild taste.

Warning Label

Keep out of reach of children. Store in a cool dry place. Do not ingest.
