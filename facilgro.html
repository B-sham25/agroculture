Facililgro Starter Fertilizer NPK 18-20-21

Facililgro Starter 18:20:21 + TE is a high-phosphorus water-soluble fertilizer with balanced NPK and trace elements. Promotes strong root development, early growth, and vigorous seedlings. Ideal for transplanting vegetables, flowers, fruits, and nursery plants in Mauritian soils.

Mode of Use

Dissolve 5–10 g per 10 L water (adjust per crop). Apply as foliar spray or soil drench during seedling stage and early growth. Use every 7–14 days or at transplanting. Water plants before and after application for best absorption. Avoid foliar spray in strong midday sun to prevent leaf burn.

Warning Label

Keep out of reach of children. Store in a cool, dry place away from food. Avoid eye/skin contact and wear gloves when mixing. Do not ingest. Rinse with water if contact occurs. Seek medical advice if irritation persists.
