Poly Mix NPK 20-10-10 +1MgO +TE

Poly Mix NPK 20-10-10 +1MgO +TE is a high-nitrogen soluble fertilizer rich in poly-phosphates, magnesium, and trace elements. Accelerates vegetative growth, leaf development, and overall plant health. Perfect for vegetables, fruits, flowers, herbs, aromatic plants, and lawns in Mauritius.

Mode of Use

Dissolve 5–10 g per 10 L water (adjust per crop). Apply as foliar spray or soil drench every 7–14 days during active growth. Use early morning or late afternoon to avoid leaf burn. Water plants before and after application for best uptake. Do not exceed recommended rates to prevent nutrient imbalance.

Warning Label

Keep out of reach of children. Store in a cool, dry place away from food. Avoid eye/skin contact and wear gloves when mixing. Do not ingest. Rinse with water if contact occurs. Seek medical advice if irritation persists.
