Cucumber Seeds

Premium cucumber seeds yield crisp, refreshing fruits full of hydration and mild flavor. These vigorous vines produce abundant smooth or ridged cucumbers perfect for fresh salads, pickles, sandwiches, or juicing in warm, sunny Mauritian gardens.

Mode of Use

Sow cucumber seeds 1–2 cm deep in fertile, well-drained soil or seed trays. Keep soil moist and warm (20–30°C) for fast germination in 5–10 days. Transplant or thin seedlings to 40–60 cm apart in rows or hills, provide full sun, trellis vines for better airflow and yield, and water consistently to prevent bitterness and promote straight, juicy fruits.

Warning Label

Keep out of reach of children. Store in a cool dry place. Do not ingest.
