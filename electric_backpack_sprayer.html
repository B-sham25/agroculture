Electric Backpack Sprayer

20L capacity with built-in electric stirring/mixing function (the "MIXING" label with tornado symbol indicates an internal agitator to keep solutions uniform, great for fertilizers or suspensions that settle). It's a modern battery-operated DC sprayer (no manual pumping needed), with a long lance/nozzle.

Mode of Use

Fill the tank with the desired liquid (pesticide, herbicide, fertilizer mix, or water) up to the marked capacity of 20L. Charge the battery fully, turn on the switch, and activate the stirring function if mixing suspensions or fertilizers. Adjust the nozzle for fine mist or coarse spray, wear protective gear (gloves, mask, boots), and apply evenly to crops, weeds, or plants from a distance of 0.5–1.5 m while walking steadily. Clean the tank, wand, and nozzle thoroughly with water after each use to prevent clogging and maintain performance.

Warning Label

Keep out of reach of children. Toxic residues may remain. Handle with care. Always unplug or disconnect and remove the battery pack first to prevent shorts or damage before cleaning.
