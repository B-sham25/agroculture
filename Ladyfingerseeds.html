Ladyfinger Seeds

Premium ladyfinger seeds produce tender, ridged pods with a mild, earthy flavor. These heat-loving plants yield abundant green okra perfect for curries, stir-fries, soups, frying, or pickling, thriving in hot, sunny Mauritian conditions with quick harvests.

Mode of Use

Sow ladyfinger seeds 1–2 cm deep in fertile, well-drained soil after the last frost risk, spacing 30–45 cm apart in rows 60–90 cm apart. Keep soil warm (25–35°C) and moist for germination in 7–14 days. Provide full sun, water consistently during flowering and pod development, and harvest young pods every 2–3 days at 8–12 cm long for tender texture and continuous production.

Warning Label

Keep out of reach of children. Store in a cool dry place. Do not ingest.
