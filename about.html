About Us

Who We Are

Agroculture Island Ltd was established in 2014 and is strategically located in Bois Cheri. We operate in the agro-chemical sector, supplying a wide range of agricultural products and solutions to support the farming community across Mauritius.

Our Vision & Mission

Our vision is to become a market leader in the coming years by continuously delivering excellence. Our mission is to serve the planters community by providing high-quality products and reliable services that support sustainable agricultural growth.

What We Offer

We provide a comprehensive range of agricultural products including herbicides, fungicides, insecticides, soluble and mineral fertilisers, seeds, bio-pesticides, and irrigation tools. Our equipment range includes manual, motorised, and electric sprayers, as well as hydroponic systems. Our target market includes sugarcane planters, vegetable growers, farmers, golf courses, hotels, and hydroponic cultivators.

Our Values

We are guided by strong corporate values including integrity, honesty, commitment, and discipline in everything we do.
