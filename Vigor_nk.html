Vigor NK

Vigor NK is a liquid nitrogen-potassium fertilizer that enhances flowering, fruiting, and crop quality. Promotes strong stems, better fruit set, and improved taste/color — ideal for mid-to-late season application on vegetables, fruits, and field crops in Mauritian agriculture.

Mode of Use

Dilute 5–10 ml per liter of water for foliar spray or 1–2 L per hectare for soil application. Apply during flowering and fruit development stages. Spray evenly on leaves or drench soil, preferably early morning or evening. Use every 10–15 days or as crop needs dictate. Shake bottle before use.

Warning Label

Keep out of reach of children. Store in a cool, dry place. Avoid eye/skin contact — wear gloves when handling. Do not ingest. Rinse with water if contact occurs. Seek medical advice if irritation persists.
