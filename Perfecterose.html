Perfectectose

Perfectectose is an amino acid-based foliar biostimulant with 2–3% total protein nitrogen and 11–12% free amino acids. Boosts plant vigor, stress resistance, nutrient uptake, and growth. Ideal for vegetables, fruits, and crops under heat or nutrient stress in Mauritian farms and gardens.

Mode of Use

Dilute 2–5 ml/L water for foliar spray. Apply during early vegetative growth, flowering, or stress periods (e.g., heat, drought). Spray evenly on leaves early morning or late afternoon to avoid leaf burn. Use every 10–15 days or as needed. Shake bottle before mixing and combine with compatible fertilizers if desired.

Warning Label

Keep out of reach of children. Store in a cool, dry place. Avoid eye and skin contact — wear gloves during handling. Do not ingest. Rinse skin or eyes with water if contact occurs. Seek medical advice if irritation persists.
