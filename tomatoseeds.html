Tomato Seeds

Premium tomato seeds yield juicy, flavorful fruits bursting with vitamins and rich taste. These vigorous plants produce abundant clusters of round or plum-shaped tomatoes, perfect for fresh salads, sauces, cooking, or home canning in sunny Mauritian gardens.

Mode of Use

Sow tomato seeds 0.5–1 cm deep in seed trays or directly in fertile, well-drained soil. Keep soil moist and warm (20–30°C) for germination in 5–10 days. Transplant seedlings outdoors after 4–6 weeks, spacing 45–60 cm apart in rows, stake or cage plants for support, and provide full sun with regular watering and feeding for healthy growth and heavy yields.

Warning Label

Keep out of reach of children. Store in a cool dry place. Do not ingest.
