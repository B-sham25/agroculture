Green Rubber Boots

Heavy-duty green rubber gumboots (Wayne brand) provide waterproof protection and durable comfort. Tall design with reinforced toe and non-slip sole. Essential for muddy fields, irrigation, rainy weather, or wet soil work in Mauritian farms and gardens.

Mode of Use

Wear over socks for comfort during fieldwork, planting, or wet conditions. Clean with water and mild soap after use, dry away from direct heat, and store upright in a cool, dry place to prevent cracking and maintain shape.

Warning Label

Keep out of reach of children. Do not expose to extreme heat or open flames. Not for chemical immersion. Rinse promptly after contact. Inspect for cracks before each use.
