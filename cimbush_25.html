CIMBUSH 25 Insecticide

Cimbush 25 is a broad-spectrum pyrethroid insecticide with cypermethrin 25% EC for rapid knockdown and residual control of chewing and sucking pests. Highly effective against aphids, thrips, caterpillars, beetles, and whiteflies on vegetables, fruits, sugarcane, and field crops in Mauritian agriculture.

Mode of Use

Dilute 1–2 ml per liter of water (follow crop-specific label rates). Shake bottle well, add to sprayer tank with water first, agitate thoroughly, and apply as foliar spray at early pest infestation. Use fine mist nozzle for even coverage, avoid windy conditions or flowering periods, and reapply every 7–14 days as needed. Wear full PPE during mixing and application.

Warning Label

POISON – WHO Class II. Keep out of reach of children. Toxic if swallowed, inhaled, or absorbed through skin. Wear gloves, mask, goggles, and protective clothing. Do not eat, drink, or smoke while handling. Wash thoroughly after use. In case of poisoning, seek immediate medical help and show this label.
